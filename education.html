Fullstack Academy of Code

New York, NY | July 2017 - October 2017

Fullstack Academy is a software engineering bootcamp that teaches a Javascript focused stack. The program consists of 2 sections: junior and senior phase. Junior phase consists of completing pair-programming wokshops that teach students the NERD stack (Node.js, Exrpess.js, React/React-Redux, PostgreSQL databases). Senior phase is the chance for students to use the stack they learned to create various projects both individually and in teams of 4, allowing us to practice AGILE development. Completed projects and presentations can be found in the "Projects" section of this site.
